About US

Vision

Expressing complex emotion in the human face is the subject for Canvas Creations painting. Growing up in the northern part of Jharkhand, Rahul Prasad has developed a close kinship with the natural world and its connection with the indigenous people. That kinship is the basis for the portraits he paints.

Canvas Creations artist works in acrylic, watercolor, and oil-medium with a bold color palette and fluid style to give his portraits a presence that captures the character of the person. Several original works of the artist are in possession of private collectors from various parts of the world.

Rahul is now open accepting commissioned work for custom portraits. Please visit commissioned work for more information.

Canvas Creation Academy is a premier institution dedicated to nurturing artistic talent and creativity. Our academy offers a diverse range of courses in painting, digital art, graphic design, and multimedia. We provide hands-on training with state-of-the-art tools and technologies for both traditional and digital art forms. Our mission is to empower students to express their unique artistic vision and achieve their creative potential. The academy is staffed by experienced instructors who are industry professionals and passionate educators. Students benefit from personalized mentorship and guidance tailored to their individual artistic goals. We offer flexible learning options, including part-time and full-time programs, to accommodate different schedules. Our curriculum includes foundational art techniques as well as advanced topics like art theory and digital media. Canvas Creation Academy fosters a collaborative environment where students can network and learn from peers. We organize regular workshops, masterclasses, and guest lectures with renowned artists and designers. Our state-of-the-art facilities include well-equipped studios, computer labs, and creative spaces. Students have the opportunity to participate in exhibitions and showcase their work to the public. We emphasize the development of a professional portfolio to help students in their future careers. Canvas Creation Academy is committed to staying current with industry trends and technological advancements. Our graduates go on to work in various creative fields, including fine arts, advertising, and entertainment.

We offer career support services, including job placement assistance and internship opportunities. The academy values diversity and inclusivity, welcoming students from all backgrounds and skill levels. Our goal is to cultivate a supportive community that inspires innovation and artistic exploration. Canvas Creation Academy believes in the transformative power of art and its ability to impact society positively. Whether you're an aspiring artist or a seasoned professional, Canvas Creation Academy is the place to refine your skills and ignite your creative passion.
